It's a me, Mario

Mario, created by Nintendo, first appeared as "Jumpman" in the 1981 arcade game "Donkey Kong." Renamed Mario in 1982, he gained fame with the 1985 release of "Super Mario Bros." for the NES, which popularized platform gaming and introduced the Mushroom Kingdom. Over the years, Mario has starred in numerous sequels and spin-offs, including "Super Mario 64," which revolutionized 3D platforming. His cultural impact is immense, spanning various game genres like racing and sports, and he remains a beloved character with ongoing success in modern titles like "Super Mario Odyssey."
